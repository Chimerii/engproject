ANGEL'S WING

The world of the angels is beautiful and pristine. I, too, was part of this world.

"Dear knight, you must understand: In the face of other angels and their great world, my punishment has made me akin to a worm."

"I can only wriggle and reach out blindly in hope that you will return to me what was once mine. I have wandered aimlessly before, and after hearing of you, I kneel before you as my savior or my end."

“Didn’t you pass by the refugees on your way to meet me? The ruined towns, the burned crop-fields?”

“Last night my army and I fell asleep in a barn half-burned by the warlord. The barn was owned by a kind man who lent it to us after we stopped the fire and saved his livestock. I did so with the strength of this wing.”

“You’re a stranger to me. I’ve never met you nor heard of you. There’s no indication of your heroism or right to take this from me. With great respect, I don’t owe you anything!”

"But are you not a mortal…? You will die and so will the person you saved. My wing will perish with you, and my life will be eternally miserable."

The Knight refused my words.

"If you are not sure of my morality, let me prove it to you. I shall follow you and assist your army."

”First, tell me this… How did you lose your wing in the first place?”

I bit my tongue. I could not answer truthfully.

”I need any help for my campaign I can take. Follow me, angel, but know that your silence will make me doubt your virtue. If we defeat the warlord together, then I’ll give you my wing.”
